Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pharmaceutical services.

What types of medicines do you import?

We import a wide range of pharmaceutical products including vaccines, antibiotics, cardiology medicines, diabetology drugs, and pediatric formulations. All our imports come from WHO-GMP certified manufacturers and comply with Nepalese regulatory standards.

How do you ensure the quality of imported vaccines?

We implement a rigorous quality assurance process that includes verifying manufacturer credentials, checking product documentation, conducting visual inspections, and ensuring proper cold chain maintenance. All vaccines undergo strict temperature monitoring throughout transit.

What is your delivery timeline within Nepal?

Delivery timelines vary based on location. Within Kathmandu Valley, we typically deliver within 24-48 hours. For other major cities, delivery takes 3-5 business days. Remote areas may take up to 7-10 business days depending on accessibility and weather conditions.

Do you handle temperature-sensitive pharmaceutical products?

Yes, we specialize in cold chain logistics for temperature-sensitive products like vaccines, insulin, and certain biologics. Our fleet includes refrigerated vehicles and we use temperature-monitored containers to ensure products maintain required temperatures throughout transit.

How can I become a distributor for your products?

We welcome partnership inquiries from licensed pharmaceutical distributors. Please contact our partnership department through the contact form above or call us directly. We'll schedule a meeting to discuss requirements, territories, and partnership terms.

What are your payment terms?

Our standard payment terms are 50% advance payment and 50% upon delivery for new clients. For established clients with good payment history, we offer credit terms of 30 days. We accept bank transfers, demand drafts, and other secure payment methods.
